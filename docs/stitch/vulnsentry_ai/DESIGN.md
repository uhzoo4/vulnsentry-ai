# VulnSentry AI
## Philosophy
Your machine's immune system.
VulnSentry is an editorial cybersecurity experience.
Not a dashboard. Not a SaaS. Not cyberpunk.

## Visual Direction
- **Background**: Deep midnight blue (#020611, #071320, #0D1B2A).
- **Accents**: Burnt Amber, Soft Ivory, Steel Blue, Pale Silver.
- **Lighting**: Large radial gradients, subtle volumetric glow. Cold intelligence.
- **Composition**: Large breathing negative space. Left-weighted typography (45% width) balanced by a floating card.

## Typography
- **Display**: Cormorant Garamond Light (8rem - 11rem, 0.82 line-height).
- **Editorial**: Serif (Canela-style).
- **Body**: Inter.
- **Rules**: Thin italics, large contrast, Roman weights. No tech fonts.

## Motion & Spacing
- **Easing**: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1).
- **Duration**: 800ms – 1400ms.
- **Philosophy**: Movement serves information. Sections overlap by 20%.
- **Scroll**: Fluid transitions, no snap scrolling.

## Emotional Goal
"My computer is alive." Professional, luxury, architectural.
